Main Site - Global Tourist Attraction Survey System

This is the central hub designed to seamlessly connect you to various facets of the GTASS platform.

Functions of Every Button:

  1. Submit Interested Place

    This feature allows users to share travel destinations they've enjoyed or wish to visit. By clicking this button, you're invited to contribute to the growing list of must-see places, enriching the community's travel options.

  2. Place Voting History

    Access your personal voting record through this function. It provides an overview of the destinations you've supported, allowing you to revisit your past preferences and reflect on your travel interests.

  3. Places

    Dive into a comprehensive collection of submitted travel spots. This area is not just a repository of destinations but also a dynamic space where you can:

    • Filter: Narrow down the list based on specific criteria like location, popularity, or type, making it easier to discover places that align with your travel desires.
    • Vote: Engage with the community by voting for your favorite destinations. Each vote helps highlight top travel spots, guiding fellow users in their travel planning.
  4. Place Popularity Analysis

    Discover the top 10 attractions by votes, providing insights into popular destinations through interactive visualizations.

  5. Quit System

    Click this button to exit the system and save data.
